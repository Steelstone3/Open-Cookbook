# Beef Kofta Curry

> Serves 2

## Ingredients

- Beef mince
- Rice

- Chilli
- Tomatoes

- Butter
- Honey
- Garam masala **OR** Mixed spice
- Ginger powder
- Coriander
- Tumeric

- Coconut milk 200g
- Rogan Josh curry paste

## Sauce

- Add to pan
  - Add coconut milk half a can (200 grams)
  - Add Rogan Josh curry paste 2 big spoons

## Rice

- Rice cooker
  - Add rice 2 150ml Measures
  - Add water 2 150ml Measures
  - Add garden peas 200 gram

## Preparation

- Get a pan
- Defrost beef mince 400 gram
- Cut a chilli
- Get tomatoes 200 gram
- Prepare the rice

## Meal

- Brown beef mince
  - Add oil
  - Add butter
  - Add seasoning
  - Add honey
  - Add garam masala **OR** mixed spice
  - Add ginger powder
  - Add coriander
  - Add tumeric
- Once brown
  - Add chilli
  - Add tomatoes
- Cook the rice
- Add the sauce

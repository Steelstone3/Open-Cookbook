# Curry

> Serves 2

## Sauce

- Get a bowl
- Measure out 300g of tinned tomatoes
- Add two big spoons of curry paste

## Meal

- Get a pan
- Defrost chicken 400 gram
- Cut half a courgette
- Cut half a pepper
- Brown chicken
  - Add oil
  - Add butter
  - Add seasoning
- Once brown
  - Add courgette
  - Add pepper
- Add sauce to the pan
- Cook until sauce is warm

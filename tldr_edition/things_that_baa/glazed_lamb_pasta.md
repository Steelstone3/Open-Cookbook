# Glazed Lamb Pasta

> Serves 2

## Preparation

- Get pan
- Get saucepan
- Get measuring jug
- Boil kettle
- Defrost lamb 400gram
- Cut one chilli
- Measure out 200g peas
- Measure out 200g of tomatoes
- Measure out 200g of pasta

## Meal

### Sauce Pan

- Measure out 250ml boiling water
- Add water to saucepan
  - Add peas
  - Add pasta
- Boil for around 15 minutes

### Pan
  
- Brown lamb
  - Add oil
  - Add butter
  - Add seasoning
  - Add garlic paste
  - Add honey
  - Add balsamic vinegar
  - Add rosemary
  - Add tomatoes
- Strain sauce pan
  - Add pasta & peas to pan
  - Mix together in pan

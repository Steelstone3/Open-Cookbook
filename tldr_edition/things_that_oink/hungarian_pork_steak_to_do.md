# Hungarian Pork Steak

> Serves 2

## Sauce

- Get a bowl
- Mix the following
  - Chopped tomato
  - 1 big spoon balsamic vinegar
  - 1 little spoon praprika


## Meal

- Defrost pork
- Cut half a pepper
- Cut 1 carrot
- Cut 1 apple
- Brown pork + butter + seasoning + garlic paste
- Add vegtables
- Add sauce
- Add coriander
